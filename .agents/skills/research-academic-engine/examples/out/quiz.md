# Skill formation quiz

**1. Skill complementarity means…**  _(remember)_

   A. early skills raise returns to later investment
   B. skills decay without practice
   C. only genetics determine skills
   D. schooling fully mediates earnings

**2. Perry Preschool effects were detectable in adulthood.**  _(understand)_

   A. True
   B. False

**3. State one policy implication of skill complementarity.**  _(apply)_


---

## Answer Key

1. **A** — Early skills raise the productivity of later investment. [Cunha & Heckman 2007]
2. **True** — Earnings/crime outcomes tracked to adulthood. [Heckman et al. 2013]
3. **Invest early; early gains compound.** [Cunha & Heckman 2007]
